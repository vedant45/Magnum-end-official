About Us

Magnum Network since its inception from 1993 has been a pioneer into Networking and power conditioning. The state of Chhattisgarh has seen many Banking, Insurance and Telecom sectors Networked in Chhattisgarh by way of Magnum's expertise and in turn revolutionise the digitisation for the state. Some of the esteemed clientele are State Bank , Central Bank , Syndicate Bank , Andhra Bank and many more. Magnum Network with its office in Bhilai in the steel city of Chattisgarh has carved a niche also in the Solar panel Installation Industry since 2015. The Company has had the privilege to successfully deliver Premium Projects pan India and has the record of quality output in the Installation domain for the Solar Industry.
